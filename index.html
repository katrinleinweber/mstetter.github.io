Welcome to my page

I am a Phd student, programmer and amaranth breeder. Here I would like to give you some insights into my different projects.

Science

Science is my first passion. I am a PhD student in the group Karl Schmid at the University of Hohenheim. There I study the domestication history of amaranth and try to elucidate the species relationship in the Amaranthus genus

Programming

PhenoType is my first large programming project. PhenoTpye is a digital field book allowing you to easily record data of experiments. It runs on Android devices and is therefore very flexible. To test PhenoType for free you can download PhenoType lite PhenoType lite PhenoType is efficient and easy to use. Just try PhenoType Another app from the PhenoType family is PhenoType Camera which is a camera app that allows you to name the image files the way you want. This makes it easier to keep track of you images.

To learn more about the PhenoType projects visit PhenoType-online.com

Amaranth breeding and domestication

In my free time I grow and breed amaranth with the long term (50 years or so) goal to domesticate amaranth.
